About Us

MOORTHY TRADERS was founded in 2015 with a vision to bridge the gap between manufacturers and retailers in Salem and surrounding areas. Starting as a small trading firm, we've grown into a trusted wholesale and retail partner for construction materials, industrial supplies, and consumer goods.

Our commitment to quality, timely delivery, and fair pricing has earned us the loyalty of over 500 satisfied customers. We believe in building long-term partnerships rather than one-time transactions, ensuring mutual growth and success for all stakeholders in our supply chain.
